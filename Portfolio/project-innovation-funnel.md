# Innovation Funnel - Product Discovery System

*Structured framework managing 60+ early-stage ideas with automated prioritization*

---

## THE PROBLEM (WHY)

**Market Context:**
Porsche Digital's Vehicle Solutions team received constant stream of feature ideas from stakeholders, customers, and internal teams. No systematic way to evaluate, prioritize, or track ideas from concept to roadmap.

**Pain Points:**
- 60+ ideas floating in emails, meetings, Confluence pages with no single source of truth
- No transparent prioritization criteria - decisions felt arbitrary to stakeholders
- PM team spending 30% of time managing ad-hoc idea requests instead of building product
- Good ideas getting lost, duplicate efforts across teams

**The Opportunity:**
- Formalize innovation process to scale team capacity
- Build stakeholder trust through transparent prioritization
- Reduce PM administrative burden by 50%+
- Create data-driven decision framework for portfolio planning

**Your Unique Insight:**
> "Innovation doesn't scale through more brainstorming - it scales through systematic evaluation that makes it safe to say 'not now' without killing future possibilities."

---

## THE SOLUTION (WHAT)

**Vision:** Lightweight innovation funnel that captures every idea, evaluates objectively, and surfaces winners transparently.

**Key Features:**
1. **Centralized Idea Intake:** Single JIRA form for all product/feature proposals
2. **Automated Scoring:** Multi-criteria evaluation (user value, business impact, effort, strategic fit)
3. **Stage-Gate Funnel:** Clear stages from "Submitted" → "Evaluated" → "Backlog" → "Roadmap"
4. **Transparent Dashboard:** Real-time view of all ideas and their status for stakeholders

**Target Users:** Internal - Portfolio Managers, Product Owners, Stakeholders submitting ideas

**Differentiation:** First systematic discovery process in Vehicle Solutions portfolio, automated scoring reduces bias

---

## THE EXECUTION (HOW - STAR)

### SITUATION
Vehicle Solutions portfolio managing multiple products with no formal innovation process. Ideas came from 15+ stakeholder sources with no prioritization framework. PM team drowning in ad-hoc requests.

### TASK
Design and implement scalable innovation funnel within 3 months. Owned process design, JIRA configuration, and stakeholder rollout.

### ACTION

**Discovery:**
- Audited existing idea sources: found 60+ proposals scattered across 8 different tools
- Interviewed 10 stakeholders to understand their pain points with current process
- Researched industry best practices (Google Ventures, Spotify innovation models)

**Strategy:**
- Designed 4-stage funnel: Intake → Evaluation → Prioritization → Roadmap
- Created weighted scoring model (40% user value, 30% business impact, 20% effort, 10% strategic alignment)
- Built in JIRA for seamless integration with existing workflow

**Execution:**
- Configured custom JIRA project with intake form, automated workflows, scoring calculations
- Created visual dashboard showing funnel metrics and status for all ideas
- Designed notification system alerting stakeholders at each stage transition
- Ran 3 training sessions for 25+ team members and stakeholders
- Migrated 60+ existing ideas into new system with initial scoring

**Challenges:**
- **Stakeholder Buy-in:** Addressed by involving 5 key stakeholders in design process
- **Scoring Subjectivity:** Created evaluation rubrics with clear criteria and examples
- **Adoption Resistance:** Made submission process take <5 minutes, showed value through quick wins

### RESULT

**Metrics:**
- **Idea Management:** 60+ legacy ideas consolidated and scored in first month
- **PM Time Saved:** Reduced ad-hoc request handling from 12h/week to 4h/week (67% reduction)
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction:** Anonymous survey showed 85% felt new process was "more transparent"
- **Process Speed:** Idea evaluation time reduced from 2-3 weeks to 3-5 days

**Impact:**
- Built foundation for data-driven portfolio planning
- Created audit trail showing why ideas were accepted/rejected (compliance requirement)
- Enabled senior leadership to see innovation pipeline at a glance
- Framework adopted by 2 other portfolios within Porsche Digital

**Learning:**
- Process adoption requires demonstrating value quickly - first 5 ideas evaluated in new system became roadmap items
- Automation reduces bias but can't replace human judgment - scoring guides decisions, doesn't make them
- Transparent "no" is better than indefinite "maybe" - stakeholders appreciated closure
