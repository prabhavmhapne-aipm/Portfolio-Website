## PIONEER PILOT: AUDIO STORIES FOR NEARBY SIGHTS

*Location-aware GenAI feature generating contextual audio narratives about points of interest during drives*

### THE OPPORTUNITY

**Pioneer Program Expansion:** Building on CarPlay Assistant success, apply validated framework to second GenAI feature. Test whether Pioneer Program approach scales across different feature types and global markets.

**Dual Goals:**
1. **Validate feature to improve product quality** - Test location-based AI content generation in real driving scenarios across different cultures/geographies
2. **Create communication potential** - Position Porsche as innovator in contextual in-car experiences, generate PR through exclusive Pioneer Community feature

**The Vision:** Transform every drive into an enriched experience by delivering AI-generated audio stories about nearby landmarks through Pioneer Community validation.

### THE SOLUTION

**Feature: Voice Stories** (ChatGPT + Voice.AI integration)
- Real-time generation of contextual narratives about nearby sights based on GPS location
- Natural-sounding voice delivery optimized for in-car listening
- User-initiated queries: "Tell me about that building" or proactive suggestions

**Pioneer Program Framework:**
- **Pioneer Community:** 100 selected real customers providing direct feedback on pre-production feature
- **Time-limited rollout:** <6 months validation phase
- **Markets:** USA, Germany + UK/Scandinavia, China, Japan/South Korea
- **Budget:** ~1M€ allocated for development and pilot execution

**Customer Benefits:**
- Early access to exclusive location-aware AI feature
- Active participation in shaping production version as Pioneer Community members

### THE EXECUTION (STAR)

**SITUATION:**
Pioneer Program framework validated with CarPlay Assistant. Needed to test scalability: Can same approach work for different feature types and global markets? Audio Stories represented complex challenge: location services, LLM content generation, voice synthesis, plus cultural content relevance across markets.

**TASK:**
Define product requirements and quality framework for location-based GenAI feature. Design Pioneer Community pilot delivering both validation goals: product quality improvement and communication potential. Establish measurement systems proving feature viability within ~1M€ budget and <6 months timeline.

**ACTION:**

**Product Definition:**
- Created user stories mapping driver needs across different journey types (commute, leisure, travel)
- Designed UX journey covering feature discovery, activation, content delivery, and feedback loops
- Conducted competitor analysis examining BMW ConnectedDrive, Mercedes MBUX, and Tesla entertainment features

**Quality Framework:**
- Partnered with Quality Department to define quality criteria:
  - **Speech Quality:** Natural voice, appropriate pacing, clear pronunciation
  - **Content Truth:** Factual accuracy verified against authoritative sources
  - **Availability:** System uptime, response time, geographic coverage
  
- Established KPI tracking framework:
  - **Adoption Rate:** % of eligible users activating feature
  - **Frequency of Use:** Average sessions per user per week
  - **Customer Satisfaction:** NPS score and in-app ratings
  - **Session Duration:** Average listening time per story
  - **Abandonment Rate:** % of stories skipped before completion

**Output Quality Tracking:**
- Defined technical quality metrics:
  - **Latency:** Time delay from user query to audio playback (<3s target)
  - **Error Rate:** % of incorrect or invalid responses (<5% target)
  - **Accuracy:** Content precision against baseline expectations (>90% target)
  - **Safety Score:** Harmful content filtered (>99% target)
  - **Data Relevance:** Degree of information required to generate neutral, engaging stories

**Cost & Resource Planning:**
- Developed cost estimation model covering:
  - ChatGPT API usage costs for 100 users over 6 months
  - Voice.AI integration and hosting infrastructure
  - Quality assurance and content validation resources
  - Program management and cross-functional coordination

**RESULT:**

**Validation Goals Achieved:**
- **Product Quality:** Comprehensive quality framework enabling structured validation with 100 Pioneer Community members across 4+ markets
- **Communication Potential:** Positioned Porsche as automotive AI innovator, Pioneer Community approach generates customer advocacy and PR value
- De-risked 1M€ investment through structured validation before production commitment

**Framework Delivery:**
- Pioneer Pilot documentation approved by stakeholders (Marketing, Business, Quality, Engineering)
- Quality criteria and KPI framework adopted as reusable template for future GenAI features
- Cost model provided financial predictability for scaling decisions

**Strategic Impact:**
- Validated Pioneer Program framework scales across different feature types and global markets
- Created repeatable methodology for pre-production validation with real customers
- Pioneer Community approach transforms customers into co-creators and brand advocates
- Enabled early go/no-go decisions: collect feedback before full rollout or kill product when adoption rate insufficient - preventing wasted production investment

**Key Insight:**
> "Pioneer Programs deliver dual value: rigorous validation for product teams and exclusive access for customers. This combination creates advocates who champion features at launch, amplifying communication potential beyond traditional marketing."
