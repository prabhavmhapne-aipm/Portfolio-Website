# IIoT SCADA KPI-Monitoring Dashboard

*Real-time industrial monitoring system reducing energy costs by 20% and downtime by 40%*

---

## THE PROBLEM (WHY)

**Market Context:**
Chemical manufacturing plants operate with legacy control systems lacking real-time visibility. Plant managers fly blind between scheduled reports, missing optimization opportunities and reacting to problems too late.

**User Pain Points:**
- No real-time visibility into equipment performance across multiple production lines
- Energy waste invisible until monthly utility bills arrive
- Downtime causes cascade through production schedule but root causes unclear
- Legacy HMI systems incompatible with modern analytics tools

**The Opportunity:**
- €150K+ annual energy costs at single plant with 20%+ waste
- Unplanned downtime averaging 8-12 hours monthly
- ATEX compliance requirements creating barriers to entry for competitors
- Customer operates 6 plants across Europe - scalable solution opportunity

**Your Unique Insight:**
> "Plant managers don't need more data - they need the RIGHT data at the RIGHT time to make decisions that prevent problems instead of reacting to them."

---

## THE SOLUTION (WHAT)

**Vision:** Real-time plant intelligence that turns reactive firefighting into proactive optimization.

**Key Features:**
1. **Unified Dashboard:** Single view of all production lines with live KPI tracking
2. **Legacy System Integration:** Connects aging PLCs and HMI systems to modern analytics
3. **Predictive Alerts:** Early warning system for equipment anomalies before failures
4. **Energy Monitoring:** Real-time visibility into consumption patterns by line and shift

**Target Users:** Plant managers, maintenance supervisors, production engineers in ATEX-compliant chemical manufacturing

**Differentiation:** First IIoT solution certified for explosive atmosphere environments, built for plants with 20+ year old equipment

---

## THE EXECUTION (HOW - STAR)

### SITUATION
Chemical manufacturer in Germany needed better visibility into production efficiency. Plant operated 24/7 with legacy equipment in explosive atmosphere (ATEX Zone 1), making traditional IoT solutions ineligible. Existing HMI systems from 2000s with no connectivity.

### TASK
Develop and deploy real-time monitoring dashboard integrating legacy PLCs and modern analytics. Owned end-to-end solution from requirements to commissioning within 6-month timeline.

### ACTION

**Discovery:**
- Spent 2 weeks on plant floor shadowing shift supervisors to understand decision-making process
- Mapped all existing data sources: 12 PLCs, 8 HMI stations, manual logbooks
- Interviewed 15+ plant personnel identifying top pain points

**Strategy:**
- Prioritized energy monitoring first (clearest ROI), then equipment health, then production tracking
- Selected SCADA platform with ATEX certification and OPC-UA compatibility for legacy systems
- Designed phased rollout: Line 1 pilot → full plant → European rollout

**Execution:**
- Integrated 12 legacy Siemens PLCs using OPC-UA protocol adapters
- Built custom dashboards with 15+ real-time KPIs per production line
- Implemented automated alert system for critical thresholds
- Deployed ATEX-certified hardware in hazardous zones
- Trained 3 shifts of operators on new system

**Challenges:**
- **Legacy Protocol Compatibility:** Solved by implementing protocol converters and middleware
- **ATEX Compliance:** Worked with certified vendors, extensive documentation for safety audits
- **User Adoption:** Designed intuitive UX, conducted hands-on training per shift, gathered feedback for iterations

### RESULT

**Metrics:**
- **Energy Reduction:** 20% decrease in consumption (€30K+ annual savings at single plant)
- **Downtime Reduction:** 40% fewer unplanned stops through predictive maintenance alerts
- **Decision Speed:** Plant managers make optimization calls in minutes vs. hours

**Impact:**
- ROI achieved in 14 months (faster than projected 18 months)
- Customer ordered 3 follow-on projects for European plants (multi-year revenue stream)
- Became reusable product template for 10+ subsequent industrial IoT projects

**Learning:**
- Industrial users trust systems they helped design - co-creation builds adoption
- Legacy integration is 70% of IIoT project complexity, but opens massive market
- Simplicity wins in 24/7 operations - operators need instant clarity, not fancy features
